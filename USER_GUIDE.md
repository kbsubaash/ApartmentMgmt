## 📋 Main Features

### 1. Dashboard

The home page shows:
- **Latest Circulars**: Recent announcements from committee
- **Pending Polls**: Upcoming decisions you can vote on
- **Complaints Status**: Tracking of your submitted complaints
- **Community Contacts**: Quick access to building services
- **Notifications**: All updates and alerts

### 2. Circulars (Announcements)

**What are circulars?**
Circulars are official announcements from the association committee.

**How to Use**:
1. Click **Circulars** menu
2. See list of all published circulars
3. Click each to read full details and attachments
4. **Sign**: Digital signature option available
5. **Download**: Save attachments (PDFs, Word docs, images)

**Digital Signature**:
- Click "Sign Circular" button
- Your signature is recorded
- Timestamped proof of approval
- No handwriting needed - just click!

### 3. Polls & Voting

**What are polls?**
Polls let members vote on building decisions without in-person meetings.

**How to Vote**:
1. Click **Polls** menu
2. See active polls you haven't voted on
3. Read the question and options
4. Click your choice (For/Against)
5. Click **Submit Vote**
6. Your vote is recorded and counted

**Poll Types**:
- ✅ Building expense approval
- ✅ Rule changes
- ✅ Maintenance decisions
- ✅ Event planning

**Results**:
- See live voting results
- Check percentage breakdown
- View voting status (open/closed)

### 4. Complaints & Requests

**Submit a Complaint**:
1. Click **Complaints** menu
2. Click **New Complaint**
3. Select category:
   - Maintenance issue
   - Noise complaint
   - Parking issue
   - Other
4. Describe the problem
5. Add photos (optional)
6. Click **Submit**
7. You get a reference number

**Track Your Complaint**:
1. Go to **Complaints**
2. See status: Open, In Progress, Resolved
3. View updates and comments
4. See resolution timeline

### 5. Members Directory

**View Other Residents** (if you have permission):
1. Click **Members**
2. Search by name or flat number
3. View available contact information
4. Note: Personal details only visible to committee members

### 6. Community Contacts

**Quick Access to Services**:
1. Click **Community Contacts** or check dashboard banner
2. See information for:
   - Electrician
   - Plumber
   - Carpenter
   - Postman
   - Hospital info
   - Police, Fire Brigade
   - Auto rickshaw driver
   - Local shops and services
3. Click to view phone and address

### 7. Profile Management

**Update Your Profile**:
1. Click **Profile** (top right corner)
2. Update personal information:
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Mailing address
3. Click **Save Changes**

**Note**: Committee members can see your contact info. Residents cannot.

## 🔔 Notifications

### Types of Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:
- ✉️ **New Circular**: When committee publishes announcement
- ✉️ **Poll Update**: New poll posted for voting
- ✉️ **Complaint Status**: Changes to your complaint
- ✉️ **System Alert**: Important system updates

### How to Receive Notifications

**Browser Notifications**:
- In-app notifications (top right)
- Mark as read or delete

**Email Notifications**:
- Check your inbox
- Check spam folder too
- Can be enabled in settings

### Notification Preferences

1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Choose what to receive
3. Select frequency (immediate, daily digest, etc.)

## 🔐 Security & Privacy

### Password Security

- **Change Password**: Settings → Security → Change Password
- **Never share** your password with anyone
- Use a **strong password**: Mix of letters, numbers, symbols
- **Minimum 8 characters** required

### Logout

Always logout when done, especially on shared computers:
- Click your name (top right)
- Click **Logout**

### Personal Information

Your information is protected:
- ✅ Encrypted transmission (HTTPS)
- ✅ Secure database
- ✅ Only authorized users can view
- ✅ Audit logs track access

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Can't Login?

**Problem**: "Invalid email or password"
- **Solution**: 
  1. Check email spelling
  2. Reset password if forgotten
  3. Check CAPS LOCK is off

**Problem**: "Account not found"
- **Solution**: 
  1. Ask admin to create your account
  2. Use email provided by admin

### Page Not Loading?

**Problem**: Blank page or error
- **Solution**:
  1. Refresh browser (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  2. Clear browser cache
  3. Try different browser
  4. Check internet connection

### Can't See Member Details?

- **Note**: Only committee members can see personal details
- If you need directory, ask committee chair

### Email Not Received?

**Check**:
1. Spam folder
2. Promotions tab (Gmail)
3. Verify email address in profile

**Contact support** if not resolved

### Forgot Your Password?

1. Go to login page
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"**
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Click link and create new password
6. Login with new password

## 📋 Common Questions

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. Encrypted connection (HTTPS), secure database, regular backups.

**Q: Can I vote multiple times?**
A: No. System allows only one vote per poll per person.

**Q: Can I change my vote?**
A: Before poll closes, you can change vote one time. Contact admin to modify closed polls.

**Q: Who can see my personal details?**
A: Only committee members. Residents cannot see each other's contact info.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, reset via email.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Currently web-based. Mobile web browser works on all phones.

**Q: Can I download documents?**
A: Yes. Click document → download button.

**Q: How long are records kept?**
A: All circulars, polls, complaints keep for archive. Deletions handled by admin.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Contact admin. Export available for authorized personal data.
